Accordion

Disclosure blocks. Open state is chevron rotation + expanded panel — no hue. Keyboard: Enter/Space toggles; each trigger is a real button.

A monochrome, text-first design system. Hierarchy comes from type, spacing, and borders — not accent color.

IBM Plex Sans and Mono ship as local woff2 under assets/fonts/ with SIL OFL 1.1. No CDN.

This demo allows multiple panels open. For exclusive mode, close siblings when one opens.
